**Title**
# U.S. Demographic & Income Analysis for Business Expansion

**Overview**
This project analyzes American Community Survey (ACS) Census data to identify high-opportunity U.S. markets for business expansion.

**Tools Used**
Python (Pandas)
MySQL
Power BI
ACS Census Data

**Business Questions**
1.Which states have strong purchasing power?
2.Which regions show workforce strength?
3.Where is education concentration highest?
4. Which markets balance affordability and opportunity?

**Key Features**
Data cleaning & transformation
SQL business analysis
Opportunity Score modeling
Interactive Power BI dashboards
Expansion market recommendations

**Dashboard Pages**
1.Introduction
2.Executive Summary
3.Workforce & Education Analysis
4.Expansion Recommendation

**Results**
The analysis identified states with strong income, employment, and education indicators as high-potential expansion markets.
